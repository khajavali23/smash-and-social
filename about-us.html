About Us

Where Sports & Social Life Come Together

Smash & Social is a modern sports and lifestyle destination designed to bring people together through sports, fitness, and community. Combining premium badminton courts, a calisthenics gym, a sports store, and a cozy café under one roof, we offer an experience that goes beyond traditional sports facilities.

Premium Badminton Courts & Academy

Professional courts, summer academy, corporate hosting, and tournaments for all skill levels. Top-quality surfaces built for accuracy, smooth movement & competitive gameplay.

Community-Driven Club Culture

Connect, learn, and play with passionate players who share the love for the sport. Meet like-minded individuals and build lasting friendships.

Training for All Skill Levels

Coaching programs designed for beginners, intermediates, and advanced athletes. Professional coaching with personalized training sessions.
